ABOUT US

We are a local surf community located in Bundoran, Ireland's surf capital. Our commitment is to bring people together to meet up for surf improvement exercises and activities on regular basis. You will be part of a community of local surfers and volunteers willing to bring Bundoran surf to professional levels!
